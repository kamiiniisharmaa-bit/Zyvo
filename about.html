About Zyvo

We believe style should be effortless, timeless, and accessible. Meet the team behind the brand.

Our Story

Zyvo was born in 2020 from a simple idea: create a curated collection of modern essentials that bridge the gap between quality and affordability. What started as a small pop-up in San Francisco has grown into a global community of people who value thoughtful design and sustainable practices.

We work directly with artisans and manufacturers who share our commitment to ethical production. Every piece is designed to last, with a focus on clean lines, versatile colors, and materials that feel as good as they look.

Our commitment: We offset 100% of our shipping emissions and use recycled packaging whenever possible. Style shouldn't cost the earth.
